Method

A simple rhythm for better financial decisions.

  1. Discover

    Understand your goals, commitments, family context and current arrangements.

  2. Design

    Compare options and trade-offs in plain language before making decisions.

  3. Implement

    Put agreed actions in place with clean documentation and follow-through.

  4. Review

    Revisit the plan as your income, priorities, family needs and markets change.
